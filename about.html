About LYOPAY

Introducing LYOPAY App, a user-friendly crypto app designed to simplify storing and managing your cryptocurrencies wherever you go, whenever you need. Created by DIGILYO APP LTD, a UK-based company, LYOPAY aims to make your crypto experience effortless and enjoyable.

We are here so that you have the ease to use cryptocurrencies all over the world.

LYOPAY was created to empower people – with a vision where we can use cryptocurrencies as our primary money. 

With LYOPAY, people get the opportunity to use cryptocurrency with the feeling of complete ownership and security.

Licensed and Regulated

LYOPAY operates in compliance with KYC, AML, OWASP, and European General Data Protection compliance and regulations. Thanks to this, all operations take place in total transparency with respect to the institutions that regulate payment flows, and safety towards customers and their funds.
